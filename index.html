Our Service

We offer all kind of investigation services to solve your problems.

Personal Investigation

Personal investigations may be conducted for a variety of reasons, such as:
to learn the truth about someone, to find a missing person,
to investigate a crime or to uncover evidence for a court case.

Corporate Investigation

Corporate investigations are investigations into the activities of companies and corporations. They are typically conducted by external private investigators when a company suspects that an employee or competitor has engaged in misconduct or illegal activity.

Forensic Investigation

Forensic investigation is the process of gathering and analyzing evidence related to a crime in order to identify and prosecute the perpetrator. This process can involve gathering physical evidence such as fingerprints, DNA, or other trace evidence.

Cyber Crime Investigation

Cyber crime investigations can involve a range of activities, from the collection of physical evidence such as computer hardware & software, to the analysis of electronic data & communications & be able to develop strategies for catching & prosecuting offenders.

Matrimonial Investigation

The investigation usually involves gathering information about a potential spouse or partner in order to assess their suitability for marriage or to uncover any evidence of infidelity or other issues that could cause problems in the marriage.

Pre-matrimonial Investigation

Pre matrimonial investigation is a comprehensive background check on a person before they enter into a marriage. This investigation is typically conducted by a professional private investigation agency and can help to uncover any information that may be relevant to a potential spouse's character, habits, financial status, and any other information that could impact the marriage.

Post-matrimonial Investigation

Post-matrimonial investigation can be helpful in situations where one partner suspects infidelity or other illegal activities, but it should be approached with caution as it can also lead to legal and emotional complications if not handled properly. It is important to consult with a reputable and experienced investigator and to follow all legal and ethical guidelines during the investigation process. It's also important to communicate clearly with your spouse and seek legal advice before taking any drastic actions based on the results of the investigation.

Fraud Investigation

Fraud investigations can be complex and require a thorough understanding of accounting practices, financial reporting, and the legal implications of any wrongdoing. It is important to work with a team of experienced investigators and legal professionals to ensure that the investigation is conducted thoroughly, ethically, and within the legal framework.

The investigation process usually involves a team of investigators who will review financial and accounting records, conduct interviews with relevant employees and stakeholders, and collect any relevant evidence to determine the extent of the fraud and identify the perpetrators.

Child maltreatment investigation

Child maltreatment investigation is the process of investigating allegations or suspicions of child abuse or neglect. Child abuse and neglect can take many forms, including physical abuse, sexual abuse, emotional abuse, neglect, and exploitation. The investigation aims to determine if the allegations are true and to protect the child's safety and well-being.

Child maltreatment investigations can be emotional and complex, and it is important to work with experienced and compassionate investigators who understand the sensitive nature of these cases. The primary goal of the investigation is to protect the child from further harm and provide them with the support and resources they need to heal and thrive.
