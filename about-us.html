Our Service

We offer all kind of investigation services to solve your problems.

Personal Investigation

Personal investigations may be conducted for a variety of reasons, such as: to learn the truth about someone, to find a missing person, to investigate a crime or to uncover evidence for a court case. if you need professional investigative services, look no further than Trinetra Contact us today!

Corporate Investigation

Corporate investigations are investigations into the activities of companies and corporations. They are typically conducted by external private investigators when a company suspects that an employee or competitor has engaged in misconduct or illegal activity.

Forensic Investigation

Forensic investigation is the process of gathering and analyzing evidence related to a crime in order to identify and prosecute the perpetrator. This process can involve gathering physical evidence such as fingerprints, DNA, or other trace evidence.

Cyber Crime Investigation

Cyber crime investigations can involve a range of activities, from the collection of physical evidence such as computer hardware & software, to the analysis of electronic data & communications & be able to develop strategies for catching & prosecuting offenders.

Matrimonial Investigation

The investigation usually involves gathering information about a potential spouse or partner in order to assess their suitability for marriage or to uncover any evidence of infidelity or other issues that could cause problems in the marriage.
