What is

Photo & Audio/Video Surveillance

Photo & Audio/Video Surveillance is a type of investigation that involves the use of various surveillance techniques, including photography, audio recording, and video recording, to collect evidence related to a particular case or investigation.

Reasons for Choosing Surveillance Services

  • Insurance claims
  • ounterintelligence
  • Abuse
  • Child Support
  • Infidelity

Types of Surveillance in brief:

  • Physical surveillance
  • Electronic surveillance
  • Covert surveillance
  • Overt surveillance
  • Technical surveillance
  • Undercover surveillance

The purpose of Photo & Audio/Video Surveillance may vary depending on the context, such as for infidelity investigations, child custody cases, or insurance fraud investigations. The evidence gathered through surveillance techniques can provide valuable information to support legal claims, help individuals make informed decisions, or assist law enforcement in criminal investigations. However, it is important to note that the use of photo, audio, video surveillance investigation can have legal and ethical implications, and investigators must adhere to the relevant laws and regulations governing the use of such techniques.
