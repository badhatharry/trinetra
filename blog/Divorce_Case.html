What is

Divorce Case Investigation

Divorce case investigation is the process of gathering evidence related to a divorce case to support a party's claims or to refuse the other party's claims..

Going through a divorce is an emotional and stressful experience, and you want to ensure that you get a fair and just settlement. Trinetra Investigation & Security Service Agency can help you navigate this difficult time with our professional and reliable divorce case investigation services. Our team of experienced investigators will gather evidence related to issues such as infidelity, hidden assets, child custody, and alimony, to ensure that you get a favorable outcome in court. With our discreet and confidential approach, you can trust us to provide you with the information and evidence you need to make informed decisions and protect your rights. Don't let your spouse take advantage of you during your divorce, contact Trinetra Investigation & Security Service Agency today for a thorough and effective divorce case investigation.

A divorce case investigation conducted by Trinetra Investigation & Security Service Agency may cover a range of aspects, depending on the specific needs and concerns of our clients. Here are some of the most common aspects that we cover in a divorce case investigation:

  • Infidelity: We gather evidence of any extramarital affairs or other forms of infidelity that may be relevant to the divorce case.
  • Hidden assets: We investigate to uncover any hidden assets that one spouse may be trying to conceal from the other spouse, such as offshore bank accounts, real estate, or other property.
  • Child custody: We gather information on the suitability of each parent for child custody, such as their parenting skills, living conditions, and any history of substance abuse or criminal activity.
  • Alimony: We investigate to gather evidence on the income and financial resources of each spouse, as well as their ability to earn income, to ensure that the alimony settlement is fair and just.
  • Property division: We investigate to gather information on the value of marital property and debts, as well as any claims of separate property, to ensure that the division of assets and debts is equitable.

Trinetra Investigation & Security Service Agency can customize our divorce case investigation services to meet the unique needs and concerns of our clients, and provide them with the evidence and information they need to protect their rights and interests during a divorce case.
