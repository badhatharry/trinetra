What is

Pre matrimonial Investigation

Pre matrimonial investigation is the process of investigating a prospective spouse's background, financial status, character, and reputation prior to marriage.

Getting married is one of the biggest decisions of your life, and you want to make sure that your partner is everything they claim to be. Types of pre-matrimonial investigation

There are several types of pre matrimonial investigation that Trinetra Investigation & Security Service Agency can conduct, depending on the specific needs and concerns of our clients. Here are some of the most common types:

  1. Background check: This involves verifying the personal, educational, and professional details provided by the prospective partner to ensure that they are true and accurate.
  2. Financial check: This involves investigating the financial stability of the prospective partner, including their income, assets, and liabilities, to ensure that they are financially responsible and stable.
  3. Social media check: This involves analyzing the social media profiles and activities of the prospective partner to identify any red flags, such as inappropriate behavior, controversial opinions, or associations with unsavory individuals.
  4. Family background check: This involves investigating the family history of the prospective partner to identify any potential issues, such as a history of criminal activity, mental illness, or substance abuse.
  5. Surveillance: This involves conducting covert surveillance of the prospective partner to gather evidence of any illicit activities or behaviors that may be cause for concern.
