What is

Loan Verification Investigation

Loan Verification investigation refers to the process of gathering and analyzing information about an Loan Verification's behavior, performance, or actions in order to make informed decisions or take appropriate actions.

If you're in the process of obtaining a loan, it's crucial to ensure that all information provided by the borrower is accurate and truthful. At Trinetra Investigation & Security Services Agency, we offer loan verification services to help you make informed lending decisions.

Here are some most common types of Loan Verification Investigations:

  • Employment Verification : These investigations focus on verifying the borrower's employment and income details, including job title, length of employment, salary or wages, and other income sources..
  • Credit History : These investigations focus on reviewing the borrower's credit history and credit score to assess their creditworthiness.
  • Property Appraisal : These investigations focus on determining the value of the property being used as collateral for the loan.
  • Identity Verification : These investigations focus on verifying the borrower's identity and ensuring that they are who they claim to be.
  • Fraud Investigations: These investigations focus on identifying and preventing loan fraud, including identity theft, falsified documents, and other forms of fraud.

Our team of experienced investigators will conduct a thorough investigation to verify the borrower's identity, employment, income, credit history, and other relevant information. We use the latest technology and investigative techniques to provide you with accurate and reliable results.

With our loan verification services, you can mitigate the risk of fraud and default, ensure compliance with regulations, and make informed lending decisions that benefit your business.
